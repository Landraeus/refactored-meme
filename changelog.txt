Hello dear builders and players! We know it has been a while, but we finally got another update for you ready!

New additions include:
- Boo Blocks, which turn rock solid when you face them!
- Bumble Bees, an enemy from Yoshi's Story that flies at you in a customizable sinusoidal pattern!
- Dice Switches and Dice Blocks, essentially ON/OFF blocks.
- More post variants and... also spikey versions of posts!
- The Wasteland tileset from world 6 of Yoshi's Island.
- A custom made Desert tileset by Cereal Lee, which includes 2 new backgrounds as well!
- A completely overhauled pause menu with many new options!
- Lots of other new tiles, decorations, misc. changes, etc.!

Naturally there's some bug fixes as well.
Happy playing and building!
